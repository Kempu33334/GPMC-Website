Meet Our Team

The passionate mathematicians and educators behind GPMO

Inferno2332

Inferno2332 is a statistics student at UNSW Sydney from the Philippines and a hardcore rhythm game addict. During high school, his greatest achievement was reaching the top 20 of the Philippine Mathematical Olympiad. As a washed and retired unc, he now shares his experience with the next generation as a problem writer at GPMO.

Kempu33334

Kempu33334 is a high school freshman who lives in Texas. As the newest addition to the GPMO commitee, he is one of the main problem writers and claims to be one of the worst at competition math there. He mainly writes algebra and number theory, since he is a A/N main. He is a participant of the OTIS program, and enjoys solving and exploring olympiad problems (more so than computational). He is a 2-time AIME qualifier and has been doing competition math for 5 years.
